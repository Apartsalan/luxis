# Brand Guidelines — Kesting Legal / Luxis

## Brand Identity

**Kesting Legal** is a modern, approachable Amsterdam-based law firm specializing in collections and insolvency law. The brand should feel professional yet warm — not intimidating like traditional law firms.

**Luxis** is the practice management platform powering Kesting Legal. It should feel modern, efficient, and trustworthy — like a premium SaaS product.

## Color Palette

> **NOTE:** The definitive brand colors should be confirmed with the user (Arsalan). The below are placeholder suggestions based on the current UI.

### Primary Colors
- **Primary Blue**: Used for main actions, links, active states
- **Dark/Charcoal**: Text, sidebar backgrounds
- **White/Light**: Backgrounds, cards

### Accent Colors
- **Success Green**: Positive states, completed items
- **Warning Amber**: Attention needed, deadlines
- **Error Red**: Errors, overdue items

### Neutral Scale
- Various grays for borders, disabled states, secondary text

## Typography

- **Headings**: Current font from the UI (to be confirmed)
- **Body**: System font stack or chosen web font
- **Monospace**: For codes, references, amounts

## Tone of Voice

- **Professional but approachable** — not stuffy legal language
- **Dutch UI** — clear, direct Dutch
- **Data-focused** — let numbers speak
- **Action-oriented** — buttons and labels tell users what happens next

## Logo & Branding

> To be provided by Arsalan — current logo, favicon, and any existing brand assets.

## Application

When creating materials for Kesting Legal or Luxis:
1. Use the defined color palette consistently
2. Apply typography hierarchy (headings, body, captions)
3. Maintain professional tone in all copy
4. Include logo placement per guidelines
5. Ensure accessibility (contrast ratios, readable fonts)
